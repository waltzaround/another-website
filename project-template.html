Design Systems + Product Design

UbiQuity is a marketing automation platform. It sends over a billion emails, texts, push notifications, forms, surveys, chatbot messages, etc every year to customers, on behalf of companies you interact with daily.



I implemented new processes, and worked on a new design system to improve a legacy platform, in a team of designers and developers. I led the design of new features, to help clients get more out of the product.

Challenge

In July 2017, the company I worked for acquired a marketing automation company called Ubiquity. The app was pretty old, and came with about 10 years of unpaid technical debt.

It suffered from an inconsistent design language, many tacked-on features caused by sales driven development, and was caught between a crossfire; too small to compete with Adobe Experience Cloud and Marketo, and too complex to compete with small business marketing offerings like MailChimp and CampaignMonitor.
