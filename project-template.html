UbiQuity
Marketing Automation

Challenge

In July 2017, the company I worked for acquired a marketing automation company called Ubiquity. The app was pretty old, and came with about 10 years of unpaid technical debt. It suffered from an inconsistent design language, many tacked-on features caused by sales driven development, and was caught in a crossfire; too small to compete with Adobe Experience Cloud and Marketo, and too complex to compete with simpler offerings like MailChimp and CampaignMonitor.


Response

To improve the usability of the app and retain customers, we binned everything and redesigned the web app with a focus on both customer, and developer experience. We introduced component + design systems pretty cheaply as the web app was transitioned from a mishmash of javascript frameworks to React.
