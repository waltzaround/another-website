UbiQuity
Marketing Automation

Challenge

In July 2017, the company I worked for acquired a marketing automation platform called Ubiquity. We fought off technical debt that blocked redesigns, an inconsistent design language making the platform difficult to understand, and a terrifying amount of sales driven development feature creep.


Response

To retain customers and to improve the usability of the platform, the team wrote off the old frontend and redesigned the platform with a focus on both customer, and developer experience.

We introduced consistent component + design systems, as well as new features pretty cheaply as the web app was transitioned from a nightmarish mishmash of javascript frameworks to React.
