Swiftly
Helping small kiwi businesses sell stuff on the internet by giving away free online stores.

Swiftly is a platform that helps small kiwi businesses start selling stuff online in 15 minutes, for free. Designed and built as part of a hackathon in 48 hours.

As of May 2020, Swiftly now serves over 750 small stores across New Zealand and has helped small kiwi businesses transact over $120,000 NZD in orders.

Website: swiftly.nz

Roles: Co-founder, Designer

Flatbot
Auto-sniping good rental listings for flatmates around New Zealand.

Designed and built a Facebook Messenger chatbot with some friends that would perform auction sniping on home rental listings. We built Flatbot to help friends and colleagues who were spending hours every day looking for a place to live.

Peaked at 100+ users on Messenger, but ended up neglected as we explored other pursuits and projects. Discontinued mid 2019.

Role: Founder, Designer

Date: Dec 2017 → Jul 2019
